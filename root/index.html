Welcome to the Toronto Blue Jays Fan Hub

About the Team

The Toronto Blue Jays are Canada’s only Major League Baseball team, known for their iconic blue uniforms, dedicated fanbase, and thrilling moments at Rogers Centre. Established in 1977, they quickly became a major force in baseball.

From winning back-to-back World Series titles in 1992 and 1993 to showcasing modern-day stars like Vladimir Guerrero Jr. and Bo Bichette, the Blue Jays are a proud symbol of Canadian baseball excellence.

Why Fans Love the Jays

Blue Jays fans are known for their loyalty, energy, and passion. Whether it's cheering from the stands or following from home, the fan culture in Toronto is unmatched. The SkyDome’s retractable roof and the CN Tower in the background make watching a game truly iconic.

With a strong farm system and exciting young talent, the future looks bright for the boys in blue. From opening pitch to walk-off home runs, every game is a must-watch experience.

  • Team Info: Founded in 1977, based in Toronto, Ontario
  • Stadium: Rogers Centre with a 49,000+ seating capacity
  • Championships: World Series Champions in 1992 & 1993
  • Key Players: Vladimir Guerrero Jr., Bo Bichette, Kevin Gausman
